THE HONEYCOMB HISTORY

Rhonda Bloch was raised in East Chicago. She is a native to northwest Indiana in which she grew up in the service industry. She is the founder of Sip Coffee House and owns Highland, Hobart, and Cedar Lake Sips.
My name is Alec and I was born raised in Crown Point, Indiana. I own the Crown Point and Cedar Lake locations after working for the Sip for 5 years.
She started off with a successful tea room called Tiffany's Tea Room. One day she realized the start rise of popularity of coffee and expanded the offerings at the tea room and then the Sip was born
I graduated from IU and I was on my way to med school when I realized I lost my dream of owning a coffee shop of my own. I grew up working in the food industry working every role a restaurant could have. Honestly, my favorite role, especially at the Sip, is being a server because of all the amazing people I meet everyday.
